Why did I create the app?

I created this app as a project for CODERHOUSE S.R.L.


The idea was create an application that could be useful to the people, so I decided to create this note-taking app, taking inspiration from Inkdrop, a note-taking app created by Takuya Matsuyama.

My idea was creating an app using MarkDown, because is a powerful language, but I didn't want my app to be as all the other apps out there, so I decided it could be a good idea to add a music player, so people could listen to they favorite music while writing notes, and well, that was my inspiration to create the logo of the app:

What can be done with the app?

This is a simple app. You can take notes like in any other app that you used.

To take notes, the first step is creating a new one, and you have 3 ways to achieve that:

  • Opening the folder and creating a new one.
  • Clicking on the button with a plus sign in the sidebar.
  • Creating a new tab on the top of the screen.

Once the note is created now you can use the magic syntax of MarkDown to make your notes beauty. You can create headings, bold and italic text, put images and links, etc.


On the other hand, you can listen to your favorite music (a max of 20 tracks) while writing your notes! They will be uploaded to our database to keep your music in the browser everytime, and for free!

Are my notes secure?

Yes! Your notes are secure. Your notes are not uploaded to any database and they are saved locally on your computer. Just make sure of not cleaning the cache of your browser if you want to keep them.


In the future I'll be adding a desktop app so you can save your notes in files and copy them :)
